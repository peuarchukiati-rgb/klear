## 1. Discord track-channel intro post

🧩 **Klear** — the clarity layer for your team + its AI.

Ever leave a hackathon huddle and realize everyone (and every AI copilot) walked away with a slightly different picture? That's **context drift** — and it's why work silently diverges mid-event. Klear kills it.

Paste your messy team chat / notes / meeting → one shared project state that every teammate AND every AI assistant reads the same way. 🤝

- 🔎 **Finds the buried decision** — Intake → Process → Store → Dispatch, Store-first, so it hunts the call that got made but never written down.
- 📂 **Living folder, not a one-shot summary** — splits state into a `companion.md` per teammate (each with a Pack Back block); paste your update back, state updates itself.
- ⚡ **Self-contained** — React + Vite, no backend, runs on your own Anthropic key right in the browser.

Built for the **Builder Experience Award** track — keep your team + its AIs reading one shared state *during* the event (which workshop to hit, who's blocked, what got decided), in real time.
